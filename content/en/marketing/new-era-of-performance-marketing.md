## The old game is over

The end of third-party cookies has rewritten performance marketing. Cookie-based attribution, audience matching, retargeting — all of them have to be redesigned from scratch.

## The new stack

- **Server-side GTM** — conversion signal on the server, not the browser
- **Conversion API** — direct event push to Meta, Google, TikTok
- **First-party data lake** — identity resolution on top of BigQuery / Snowflake
- **Consent Mode v2 + TCF 2.2** — legal compliance and modeling losses

## The Roibase approach

We tie performance marketing to **engineering discipline**. Every signal, every event, every conversion — measurable, verifiable, repeatable.
